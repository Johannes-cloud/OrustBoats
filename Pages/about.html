About this page

This page is dedicated to archiving the sailboats that were made on the Swedish island of Orust. The island is located at the western coast of Sweden, near Gothenburg. The island is known for they're sailboats that are both great bluewater and inland ships.

The boats are known for offering the best in quality and ability. This page will mainly focus on the boats that are in the 34-foot class.
